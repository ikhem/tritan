Kiva Clone

A non-profit facilitating crowdfunding loans to entrepreneur around the world.

Technology Used

React, Redux, Semantic-UI, Stripe, GoogleMaps, Moment, Node, Express, Massive, Auth0, Postgres

Kiva is a non-profit organization that facilitates crowdfunding loans to entrepreneurs around the world. I chose to clone this site as a personal project because these are the problems/issues that I think software can solve. I was able to implement the main features of adding loans and checking out loans.

Hunger Games

A fun lunch roulette app you can play with your friends and co-workers.

The hunger games is one of the first apps I developed when learning to develop in React. It randomly takes a list of your friends or co-workers, a list of resturants of your choice, then randomly picks a person and a resturant. The winner has to pay for everyones lunch at that venue.
