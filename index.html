Who Am I?

You're looking for more then a developer. You're looking for a cultural fit...

  • Full-Stack Web Developer

    Utilizing the most current web technologies I strive to build applications that not only strives to solve problems but also showcases my talents as a front-end and back-end developer.

  • A Lifelong Learner

    I think the most meaningful project you can work on is yourself. I strive to never be complacent whether it pertains to my professional development or my personal well being. You'll be hard pressed to find me not trying to improve myself in someway.

  • Colleague/Collaborator

    I get excited being personally involved in helping develop the people around me. You're only as strong as the people you surround yourself with. My ability to help them speaks just as much to my abilities as it does to theirs.

  • Professional

    I don't claim to have all the answers or can solve all the problems I come across. But my strongest attribute is my ability to ask the right questions, strive to find the best solutions, and be forward about what I do and do not know.
